About Me

Hi there! My name is Remington and I am currently a Computer Science student at Chico State University. I have been a tutor since middle school and love helping students find a passion for learning. I believe that it is a lot easier to learn about something when you genuinely care about it, so I always try to relate the subject matter at hand to a student’s interests. I am also a Cross Country runner for my college and believe that confidence in yourself, passion, and hard work are an unstoppable combination for success in any endeavor.
