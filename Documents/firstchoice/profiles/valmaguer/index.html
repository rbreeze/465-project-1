About Me

Greetings! My name’s Victoria and I am a graduate from the University of the Pacific with a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology and Sociology. Before working with First Choice Tutoring, I worked as an Assistant Manager at a Group Home for troubled youth. I really enjoy working with adolescents from all backgrounds because I believe that they are our future and we need to nurture them into successful adults! I believe each child is unique and brings something special to this world. As academic mentors, it is our job to help the youth become the best versions of themselves. We must help them develop an inner confidence to change the world!
